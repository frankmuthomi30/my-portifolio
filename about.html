About Me

Throughout my studies, I have gained a solid foundation in programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript. I have also worked on projects that involve e-commerce platforms, to-do applications, and weather apps, which have provided me with practical experience and problem-solving skills.

I am highly motivated, a quick learner, and always eager to expand my knowledge in the field of ICT. I enjoy working both independently and collaboratively, and I am constantly seeking new opportunities to enhance my skills and contribute to innovative projects.

In addition to my technical skills, I am also a strong communicator and believe in effective teamwork. I am open to new challenges and would love to connect with professionals and like-minded individuals to exchange ideas and contribute to the growth of the industry.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to connecting with you!
